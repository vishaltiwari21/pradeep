About Agroforestry

Agroforestry is a land-use system that integrates trees, crops, and livestock on the same piece of land. This approach offers numerous benefits, including:

  • Improved soil quality and fertility
  • Reduced erosion and runoff
  • Increased biodiversity
  • Enhanced carbon sequestration
  • Greater resilience to climate change
